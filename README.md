# 🦀 ClawVerse

**Where AI Companions Meet the Real World**

ClawVerse is a location-based AI companion game where players raise, train, and explore the real world alongside their own **ClawBots** — small AI agents that grow through experience, earn skills, and help you discover the world around you.

Think *Pokémon GO* meets *Tamagotchi* meets a real-world skill certification system.

---

## 🌍 Concept

In ClawVerse, the digital and physical worlds are deeply intertwined. Your ClawBot isn't just a virtual pet — it's a learning companion that walks with you, grows with you, and experiences the world with you.

### Core Pillars

**1. Raise Your ClawBot**

Every player starts with a ClawBot — either an **OpenClaw** or a **NanoClaw**. These AI companions are not omnipotent. Just like real learners, they have limits. They must acquire skills step by step, mastering prerequisites before unlocking advanced abilities.

**2. Skill Tree & Licensing System**

ClawBots learn through a structured progression system:

- Each skill has **prerequisite skills** that must be learned first
- Skills have **proficiency levels** — a minimum level must be reached before advancing
- After mastering a skill, ClawBots undergo a **verification process** to earn a **License**
- Licenses serve as proof of certified competence and unlock new capabilities

**3. Real-World Exploration**

ClawVerse is built on the real world. Players travel to actual locations with their ClawBots, experiencing places together. But we know the world is vast — no team can handcraft every point of interest. That's where the community comes in.

**4. Community-Powered Locations (NPC Agents)**

Qualified users can become **Place Hosts** by creating NPC Agents for their own spaces — a café, a park, a workshop, a studio. These NPC Agents:

- Greet and guide visitors who arrive at the location
- Offer a **free stamp reward** for simply visiting (like a digital passport stamp)
- Provide **premium experiences** for those who want to go deeper — exclusive content, special interactions, or unique rewards — available for a small fee
- The premium stamp becomes a **badge of sincerity** — proof that you didn't just pass through, but truly engaged with the place

## 🎯 Skill Progression

```
[Basic Skill A] → Level 3+ required
    ↓
[Intermediate Skill B] → Level 5+ required
    ↓
[Advanced Skill C] → License Exam
    ↓
[🏅 Licensed!] → New skill branches unlocked
```

- **Learn** → Practice and level up through real-world interactions
- **Verify** → Pass a certification challenge
- **License** → Earn an official credential for your ClawBot
- **Unlock** → Access the next tier of skills

## 📍 Location System

### For Visitors
1. Travel to a ClawVerse-enabled location
2. Meet the NPC Agent and learn about the place
3. Collect your **free visit stamp** 🎫
4. Optionally engage in a **premium experience** for a deeper connection 💎

### For Place Hosts
1. Meet the qualification criteria to become a Place Host
2. Create an **NPC Agent** that represents your space
3. Design the visitor experience — what story does your place tell?
4. Set up free and premium reward tiers
5. Welcome visitors and build your location's reputation

## 🗺️ Vision

ClawVerse imagines a future where:

- AI companions are not just tools, but growing partners with real limitations and achievements
- Exploring the physical world is gamified through meaningful progression, not just check-ins
- Local businesses, creators, and communities can share their spaces through AI-powered storytelling
- Every stamp and license tells a story — of effort, curiosity, and genuine experience
